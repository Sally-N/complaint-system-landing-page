Our Features

The online complaint management system has the following features:

Post a complaint

Post a complaint regarding a poor public service you are experiencing in your area such as poor waste disposal. You can also attach an image.

Comment on a complaint

Other users/Public service/Government accounts can comment on the posted complaints

Update Password

Complaintant allows you to update your pasword. It is our responsibility to make sure that the users acounts are accessible only to them.

About Us

Complaintant, was developed as a public platform where users can post about the issues they are facing in regards to the poor quality systems/services and ammenities offered to them by the government. In Kenya, Government institutions get away with providing less quality services to its citizens that endanger their lives. With our Online Complaint Management System, we strive to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience for both government institutions and the public alike. Our platform empowers you to manage complaints with ease, enabling you to address issues promptly and enhance government trust.
